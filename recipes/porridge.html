Kornmjölsgröt (Barley Porridge)

Description

This recipe is adapted from Trine Theut and from Över Öppen Eld Vikingatida Recept (Over an Open Fire Viking Age Recipes), with modifications.

Makes about 4 to 6 servings.

Ingredients

  • 10-15 cups of water
  • 2 cups of chopped barley kernels, soaked overnight in cold water
  • A handful of whole grain wheat flour
  • A handful of crushed hazelnuts
  • 3-4 tablespoons of honey
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Place ingredients in a large pot.
  2. Pour 10 cups of water into the pot and heat to a rolling boil.
  3. Stir regularly, reducing heat to maintain a low boil.
  4. Add water if the mixture starts getting too thick.
  5. Cook until done, about an hour.
  6. 15-20 minutes before porridge is done, add a cup of chopped fruit if you like.
  7. Serve with fresh cream or butter for a richer porridge.
