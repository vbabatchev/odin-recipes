Meat Stew

Description

This recipe is comes from Vikingars GÃ¤stabud (The Viking Feast), and makes 4 servings.

Ingredients

  • 8 cups of water
  • 1/2 kg meat of choice
  • 3-4 carrots
  • 3 yellow onions
  • 1 turnip
  • 1.5 teaspoons salt
  • Dash of black pepper
  • Thyme

Steps

  1. Season the meat with salt and pepper.
  2. Put the meat in the kettle.
  3. Pour water over the meat so it is covered and place the kettle over fire.
  4. Turn kettle every 5-10 minutes to ensure even spread of heat.
  5. Cook the meat for 1 hour after the water boils, adding water if needed to keep meat covered.
  6. Peel and cut the vegatables into pieces.
  7. Add the vegetables and thyme, and boil for 15 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
  8. Serve dish with bread.
